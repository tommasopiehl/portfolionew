    WHO AM I

    I am an independent, completely self-taught graphic designer based in Stockholm, Sweden. My art is highly influenced by cartoon-style illustration but isn't by any means limited to it's traditional frames. I am on a constant search for ways to express myself through cover-art, NFT:s, websites and anything inbetween. With no education in art but an exellent connoisseur of cartoons I like to think of my self as a man of the people regarding my status as an artist.
