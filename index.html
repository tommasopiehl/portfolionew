    WHO AM I

    I am an independent, completely self-taught graphic designer, soon to be engineer based in Stockholm, Sweden. My art is highly influenced by cartoon-style illustration but isn't by any means limited to its traditional frames. I am on a constant search for ways to express myself through cover art, NFT:s, websites, and anything in between. With no education in art but as an excellent connoisseur of cartoons, I like to think of myself as a man of the people regarding my status as an artist.
